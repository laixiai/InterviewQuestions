## Facilities Technician Developmental Program (Fixed-Term Contract) Job Skill Interpretation

### Key Responsibilities Interpretation
The core of this position is to learn and grow into a competent Facilities Technician under direct supervision. Your primary function is to support the data center team by absorbing knowledge and applying it to basic, supervised tasks. The most critical responsibilities include **shadowing senior technicians to inspect, maintain, and repair various data center systems**, such as piping and mechanical components. This hands-on learning is complemented by theoretical training modules. Another key responsibility is **performing basic maintenance, repair, and problem identification tasks by strictly following prescribed procedures**. Your value to the team is measured by your accuracy, timeliness, and the quality of your work. You are expected to be an active learner, constantly asking questions about data center operations, processes, and tools to build your technical skill set.
