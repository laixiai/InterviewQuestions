### Question 2ï¼šTell us about a time you had to learn a new, complex technical concept. How did you approach it?
*   **Points of Assessment**: Assesses your learning process, your initiative, and your enthusiasm for tackling new technical subjects.
*   **Standard Answer**: "In my computer science class, we were introduced to the concept of recursion. Initially, it was quite challenging to grasp. *   **Potential Follow-up Questions**:

### Question 4ï¼šThis role requires strong logical and abstract thinking. Can you give an example of a complex problem you solved using logic?
*   **Points of Assessment**: Evaluates your problem-solving methodology and your ability to think structurally and analytically.
*   **Standard Answer**: "In my math class, we were given a logic puzzle that involved determining the order of five people in a race based on a series of confusing, non-sequential statements. ### Question 6ï¼šHow would you explain a technical concept like an API to someone who is not technical, for instance, your grandparents?
*   **Points of Assessment**: Tests your communication skills, specifically your ability to simplify complex topics and use analogies.
*   **Standard Answer**: "I would explain it using a restaurant analogy. Imagine you are at a restaurant.
